Top 5 Horror movies

1. The Nun

Genre(s): Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Review: The Nun is so scary that YouTube had to actually ban the trailer because it was traumatizing people.

Plot twist: A priest with a haunted past and a novice on the threshold of her final vows are sent by the Vatican to investigate the death of a young nun in Romania and confront a malevolent force in the form of a demonic nun.
